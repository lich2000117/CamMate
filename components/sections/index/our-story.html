Our Mission

Cam Mate was born from a simple idea: advanced security camera intelligence should be accessible to everyone, while preserving privacy and control.

We're committed to bringing cutting-edge technology and convenience to every home owner, leveraging open source software to create solutions that are powerful yet affordable.

Our core values are:

  • Privacy as a fundamental right
  • Democratizing advanced technology
  • Supporting open source software

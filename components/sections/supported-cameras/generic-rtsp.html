Generic RTSP Support

Cam Mate supports any camera that provides an RTSP stream. This includes many other brands and models not listed above. Simply provide the RTSP URL, and Cam Mate will handle the rest.

Example RTSP URL Format

rtsp://username:password@camera-ip:554/stream1

Automatic Discovery

Cam Mate can automatically discover most RTSP cameras on your network, making setup even easier. Just connect the camera to your network and the app will find it.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2024

1. Introduction

At Cam Mate, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our intelligent AI camera assistant product and services.

2. Information We Collect

Our core design principle is local processing and minimizing data collection. We collect:

  • Camera feeds that you explicitly connect to the system
  • Device information necessary for system functionality
  • Optional account information if you choose to create an account
  • Analytics data to improve our services (which can be disabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your camera feeds are processed locally on your Cam Mate device and are not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup features. We use other collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new products and features
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. All data transmissions between your devices and our servers (when applicable) are encrypted using industry-standard methods.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to or restriction of processing
  • Data portability

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Contact Us" section on our website.
